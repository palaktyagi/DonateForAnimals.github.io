ABOUT US



Donate For Animals is helping and providing the necessary care and protection to our four-legged friends. Saving animals from distressing and abusive situations, nurturing them back to health and giving them a home needs rescue operations and rehabilitation programs for which we require your help.
Your little donation Gives Them a Forever Home, Provides for their medical care and Assistance for strays, pets.
Social Awareness is very necessary to stop animal cruelty! We are spreading awareness about human treatment towards animals, and Organizing campaigns for pet adoption and foster homes.
Use Contact US information to send updates about pets and help spread the word for adoption.
